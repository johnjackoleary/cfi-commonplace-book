***Meaning*** :: <u>U</u>ndershoot <u>N</u>orth, <u>O</u>vershoot <u>S</u>outh

***Explanation***
This is an example of [[Compass Errors]].

This acroynm helps to remember how to compensate for the [[NOSE]] compass error:
- **Undershoot North (U-N):** When turning to a northerly heading, roll out of the turn _before_ the compass reaches North (e.g., at 350° for a 360° heading).
- **Overshoot South (O-S):** When turning to a southerly heading, roll out of the turn _after_ the compass passes South (e.g., at 190° for a 180° heading).
