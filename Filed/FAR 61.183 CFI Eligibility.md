#### Key Quotes (Paraphrased)
- Hold either a [[CPL|commercial pilot certificate]] or [[ATP|airline transport pilot]] certificate with:
	- aircraft category and class rating that is appropriate
	- instrument rating
- Logbook endorsements on:
	- Knowledge areas from [[FAR 61.185 CFI Knowledge Reqs|FAR 61.185]]
	- Areas of operation from [[FAR 61.187 CFI Flight Reqs|FAR 61.187]]
- Pass knowledge tests ([[FOI]] & [[FIA]]) on areas [[FAR 61.185 CFI Knowledge Reqs|FAR 61.185]]
- Pass a [[CFI ACS|pratical test]]
- Receive an endorsement for [[Spin Lesson Plan|spin training]]
