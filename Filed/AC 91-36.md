***Key Quotes***:
* encourages pilots making VFR flights near noisesensitive areas to fly at altitudes higher than the minimum permitted by regulation
* Pilots operating noise producing aircraft... over noise sensitive areas should make every effort to fly not less than 2,000 feet above ground level ([[AGL]]), weather permitting. For the purpose of this [[AC]], the ground level of noise-sensitive areas is defined to include the highest terrain within 2,000 feet AGL laterally of the route of flight, or the uppermost rim of a canyon or valley.
