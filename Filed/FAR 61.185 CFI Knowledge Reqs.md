#### Key Quotes
A person who is applying for a flight instructor certificate must receive and log ground training from an authorized instructor on:
1. The fundamentals of instructing, including:
	1. The learning process
	2. Elements of effective teaching
	3. Student evaluation and testing
	4. Course development
	5. Lesson planning
	6. Classroom training techniques
2. The aeronautical knowledge areas for a [[FAR 61.97|recreational]], [[FAR 61.105 PPL Knowledge Reqs|private]], and [[FAR 61.125 CPL Knowledge Reqs|commercial]] pilot certificate
3. The [[FAR 61.65 Instrument Rating Reqs|aeronautical knowledge areas for the instrument rating]]
