***Meaning*** :: <u>N</u>orth-<u>O</u>pposite, <u>S</u>outh-<u>E</u>xaggerated

***Explanation***
This is an example of [[Compass Errors]].

When turning from a northerly heading, the compass briefly shows a turn in the opposite direction.

When turning from a southerly heading, the compass shows a turn in the correct direction, but at a faster rate than is actually being turned.
