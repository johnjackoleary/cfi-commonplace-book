> [!quote] From [[WINGS - Pilot Proficiency Program|WINGS]] [Accredited Activity Page](https://www.faasafety.gov/WINGS/pub/accreditedactivities/accreditedActivityViewer.aspx?aaid=%20280)
> Satisfactory completion of a Practical Test for the initial issuance of an Instrument Rating within the last 2 years meets the requirements for the Advanced Level of the **_WINGS_** - Pilot Proficiency Program and 4 of the 6 requirements for the **_Basic_** Level. 
> 
> An on-line course on the topic of Aeronautical Decision Making must be completed as part of the **_Basic_** Level. Click [here](http://www.faasafety.gov/gslac/ALC/course_catalog.aspx) to go to the Online Course Catalog.
> 
> In addition, the Takeoff and Landing Flight tasks of the **_Basic_** Level must be completed to earn the Basic Level phase.
